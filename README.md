This dashboard provides a visual overview of monthly coffee shop performance, helping track sales, order trends, peak timings, and branch-wise results in a simple, interactive format.


‚Ä¢	**Business Problem** - To deliver clear insights into revenue, order volume, popular items, and best-performing days/times, enabling management to make informed operational and marketing decisions.


‚Ä¢	**Goal of the Dashboard** - The goal is to provide a clear, interactive view of sales performance ‚Äî allowing users to compare outlet types, item categories, customer ratings, and store characteristics to support better decisions.


‚Ä¢	**Walkthrough of Key Visuals** 


1. KPI Cards display total sales, total orders, average order value, and customer trends.

2. Monthly/Weekly sales charts show how revenue fluctuates over time.

3. Branch-wise comparison visuals highlight which store performs better.

4. Daily and hourly trend visuals identify peak demand periods.

5. Category or product visuals show top-selling drinks and items.

6. Filters/Slicers allow analysis by store, date range, item category, and time slots.


‚Ä¢	**Business Impact & Insights**


1. Identifies best-performing stores and high-demand products.

2. Highlights peak hours/days to optimize staffing and inventory.

3. Supports promotional planning based on customer buying behavior.

4. Enables faster, data-backed decision making for improving revenue and customer satisfaction.
